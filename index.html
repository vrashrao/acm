ACM-SAHYADRI CHAPTER

ACM, the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.


We see a world where computing helps solve tomorrow’s problems – where we use our knowledge and skills to advance the profession and make a positive impact. ACM SAHYADRI plans and organizes a wide range of events that include technical workshops, guest lectures, online events and various other competitions, throughout the year.
